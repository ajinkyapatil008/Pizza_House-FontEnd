FAQ

How is it different from others?

We're a company that uses an innovative cryogenic process to flash freeze pizzas fresh from the oven. Real, traditional Neapolitan pizzas cooked in a wood burning oven. This allows us to maintain the taste and aroma of the pizza that “reawakens” in your home oven unaltered.

How long does it take for delivery?

Maximum 30 minutes.

What is the minimum order amount for delivery?

Minimum order amount for delivery is Rs 80 (inclusive of taxes).

How much protein it contains?

The protein content in a typical pizza varies, but it is still an excellent source of nutrition. A regular slice contains 13 to 22 grams of protein or about 25% of the recommended daily value in a 2,000-calorie diet.

Can I modify / cancel my online order?

The online order once placed cannot be modified or cancelled, either through the website or offline by calling the restaurant.
